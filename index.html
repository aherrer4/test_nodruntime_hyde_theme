Whoami

I'm an unshakable optimist, believing in technology as the key to unlock human greatest potentials. I'm just passionate about everything regarding technology, ranging from VLSI design to Frontend/Backend development, working both at the research and industry level.

I'm currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Electronics at the University of Rome Tor Vergata, working in the Network Programmability Lab, which I've joined in late 2016. Here, I carry System Research at the hardware level, building the next generation Internet trough ad-hoc FPGA-based architectures. As a spin-off, I serve as Chief System Architect at Axbryd, building the next generation datacenter networks.

I love to make available my expertise to startups, helping them build their Minimum Viable Products and offering guidance in the process. I currently serve as Frontend Developer For GenomeUp and as Backend Developer for Docunque.

I also love spreading the verb of technology, serving as Lecturer both at the University of Rome Tor Vergata and at University of Rome La Sapienza.
